The Best Movies According to Omii

My top 3 movies of all-time.

12th Fail

The inspiring story of an IPS officer who failed class 12 exams, did odd jobs, slept for just three hours each night, and started over four times to clear the UPSC exams.

Sita Ramam

Sita Ramam is nothing more than the love story of two people from different worlds falling in love while writing letters to each other.

Hi Nanna

A single father begins to narrate the story of the missing mother to his child and nothing remains the same.
